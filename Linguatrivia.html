About Linguatrivia

Put your language knowledge to the test with Linguatrivia, an interactive quiz by Linguascope. Simply select a language (French, Spanish, German, Italian, or English), choose a level of difficulty and answer questions against the clock.

With a bank of hundreds of questions on language, culture, geography, and more, Linguatrivia will put you through your paces whether you are revising for an exam or just want to practise and improve your language skills.

To access Linguatrivia online or through the app, you will need a Linguascope account. Click here for details about subscribing to Linguascope.

Linguatrivia is also the ideal tool for language teachers looking to organise competitions within their school. The website includes a Test Paper Generator that allows them to print out a sheet of randomly selected multiple-choice questions. The questions are aimed at students in their first, second or third year of studying the language.

Hundreds of schools run a LinguaTrivia competition each year, helping stimulate interest in language learning and identify and reward achievement on the part of students.
